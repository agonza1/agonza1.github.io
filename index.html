About Alberto

About

Alberto is from Barcelona, Spain and is currently living in Chicago, US. He holds a degree in Telecommunication Engineering from UPC and a Masters degree in Information Technology and Management from IIT. He is a consultant specializing in analyzing, implementing and testing IoT, VoIP and WebRTC applications and currently works as a Senior Sales Engineer at WebRTC.Ventures.

His telecom engineering background and interest in tech have led him to build many projects for small and big enterprises, projects including: video chat, telepresence remote control, speech analytics and many others Real Time applications. Alberto is known for his superior ability to multitask. His experience with a variety of products has also led him to become the expert technical voice for WebRTC.Ventures’ customers.

Alberto has been sailing since he was 8 years old. He loves kiteboarding, skiing and, although he’s not a very good dancer, he keeps trying.
